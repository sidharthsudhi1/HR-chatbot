

**Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer**

**Location:** [Bangalore, Karnataka, or Remote]

**Department:** Technology/Engineering

**Reports To:** HR Manager, ABC TECH

**Job Summary:**

The Machine Learning Engineer will design, develop, and deploy machine learning models that solve complex problems and enhance the capabilities of our products. The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in machine learning algorithms, data processing, and programming, along with experience in deploying models into production.

**Key Responsibilities:**

1. **Model Development:**
   - Research, design, and implement machine learning algorithms and models.
   - Conduct experiments to test hypotheses and validate models.
   - Optimize model performance and scalability.

2. **Data Handling:**
   - Collect, preprocess, and analyze large datasets.
   - Perform feature engineering and selection to improve model accuracy.
   - Implement data augmentation techniques when necessary.

3. **Deployment:**
   - Develop end-to-end machine learning pipelines.
   - Deploy models in production environments, ensuring reliability and scalability.
   - Monitor and maintain model performance, making adjustments as required.

4. **Collaboration:**
   - Work closely with data scientists, software engineers, and product managers to integrate models into products.
   - Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
   - Provide mentorship and guidance to junior team members.

5. **Documentation and Reporting:**
   - Document processes, methodologies, and model details thoroughly.
   - Communicate findings and results to stakeholders effectively.
   - Stay updated with the latest research and advancements in machine learning and AI.

**Qualifications:**

- **Education:** Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.
- **Experience:** 
  - Proven experience as a Machine Learning Engineer or similar role.
  - Strong programming skills in Python, R, or Java.
  - Hands-on experience with machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn).
  - Experience with data processing frameworks (e.g., Pandas, Spark).
- **Skills:**
  - Deep understanding of machine learning algorithms and principles.
  - Proficiency in using cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) for model deployment.
  - Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  - Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

**Preferred Qualifications:**

- PhD in a relevant field.
- Experience with deep learning techniques and neural networks.
- Familiarity with natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision.
- Knowledge of software development best practices, including version control (e.g., Git).

**Working Conditions:**
